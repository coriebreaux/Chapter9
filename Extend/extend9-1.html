Allen County Fair Classic Car Show

The Annual Allen County Fair starts Friday, August 10th and runs through Thursday August 16th. Sunday will feature a classic car show at the Bodmer Pavalion. See classic and antique cars from all over the country. On loan from Hollywood is the 1989 Lotus Espirit SE from the movie Pretty Woman, starring Richard Gere and Julia Robers. Also on loan are the cars from Fast and Furious and Gone in 60 Seconds.

Entertainment

Country and Music star Tim McGraw will perform Saturday, August 11th at 7:30 p.m. in the Ray Charles Auditorium. Local blue grass music band, Kettle Moraine String Band, will open for McGraw. Tickets will be $20 to $75 depending on seating location. Call the auditorium box office for details.
