Tri-State Job Fair

Job Fair

The Tri-State Job Fair runs from October 26th to October 28th from 11 a.m. to 9 p.m. daily. Jones and Laughlin Employment Services is one of the many proud sponsors of the 15th annual Jobs Fair. Area employers will have private booths set up for on-the-spot interviews. Bring extra copies of your resume. This years Job Fair will be held at Chicago's Navy Pier in the Grand Pavilion.

Mission

.The Tri-State Job Fair promises to bring together and match the best individuals with organizations. Even during difficult economic times, an organization may need a special job skill and Tri-State and Jones and Laughlin Employment Services will do their best to make those matches a reality.
