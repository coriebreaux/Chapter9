The All-State Bandmasters (ASB)

The ASB will be holding auditions for the All-State summer band competition this March. Music for various instruments has already been determined. To see music and rules for your instrument, goto www.all-stateband.com\music. Look for your particular instrument. The sheet music can be purchased through All-State Bandmasters or you through one of the links proveded on the Web site.

Miscellaneious Information

  • Auditions begin promptly at 8:30 a.m.
  • Students will audition from behind a screen
  • Students must be registered before the auditions begin, there will be no walk-ins allowed.
  • Students must demonstrate knowledge of scales and arpgeggios for their instrument without sheet music.
  • Students will be graded on a point system in four categories: Scales, Prepared Music, Tone, Site Reading.
  • Students must past with a minimum score in all four categories to be asked back for a follow-up audition.
