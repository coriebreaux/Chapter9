Exhibitors

The St. Michael's Hospital Health Fair Committee is accepting reservations from commercial, non-profit, or private vendors who wish to have food, non-profit, commercial, or information booths. Booth spaces are available from Monday September 1st through Friday September 5th, from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. All booths are indoors at the Main Pavilion of the Country Fair Grounds. Electricity is available if requested.

If you would like to reserve a booth, the deadline for reservations is Friday, September 5th. Entries must be postmarked September 5th or be hand-delivered to the County Health Office by 5 p.m. on September 5th. For booth reservation forms, contact the Health Fair Committee from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Monday through Friday at the County Health Office.
