Booth Reservations

The Tri-State Job Fair is accepting reservations from commercial and non-profit organization who wish to have presentation booths and or interview space. Interview booths will have no furniture, so employors must bring their folding tables and chairs. Booth spaces are available from Friday October 26th through Sunday October 28th, from 11 a.m. to 9 p.m. All booths are indoors at the Navy Pier Grand Pavilion. Electricity is available if requested.
